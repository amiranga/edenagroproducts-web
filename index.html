About Us
What we do

EDEN Agro-products is value added by dehydrating fresh vegetables, leaves, and fruits. The technology of dehydrating was one of the famous methods that was used by the ancient ancestors of the Sri Lankan farming community which helped to prevent foods with a long shelf-life of foods. And it is not containing any harmful chemicals when processing dehydrating foods. That quality of dehydrated value-added products catches the interest of working women in Sri Lanka who struggle with the day-to-day activities of their busy lives. Creating value responsibilities being EDEN Agro-products corporate purpose, EDEN agriculture thrives to be a corporate role model in Sri Lankan agro-products that inspires at roaches the life of every Sri Lanka by being the best solution provided in the agriculture food industry shortly.
